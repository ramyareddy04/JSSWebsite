Awards

This is a non profit event. Participating in Junior Solar Sprint provides a hands-on opportunity for students to apply Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics (STEM) concepts, creativity, and problem solving skills as they design, construct and race solar-powered cars. Participants use their imagination and ingenuity to construct vehicles powered by a standardized solar panel and motor and compete against their peers. Teams are judged on car speed, supporting evidence of their design artistic merit, and craftsmanship. Awards will be given for design in addition to the track event itself. Government and non-government organizations supporters are Department of Energy and Environment(DOEE) and Young Scholars Circle.

JSS Team Cunducted brainstorming sessions multiple times and also distributed the Solar Kits for free of cost for all the registered teams.
